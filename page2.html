Joys and Annoyances of Riding

Riding motorcycles can be an extrememly relaxing, rewarding, and fun experience. However, it can also come with some downfalls.

Riding calls for a lot of motor coordination and external processing skills. With practice, you gain a high level of experience with your coordination and it becomes extremely rewarding and enjoyable to test your riding skills while out on the street, say by practicing leaning over and trying to take an exact line through a turn.

Even though the joys of riding are most promplty remembered, the annoyances of riding a motorcycle can be numerous. Most commonly, maintenance of the bike is time-consuming and costly.
